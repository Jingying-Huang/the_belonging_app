Making Friends as a Commuter commute, socializing, academics, making friends For me the most difficult part of coming to college was not making many friends because of my living situation. Since I commute to UC Davis from Sacramento, I never experienced the dorm life. Many of my closest friends were from high school and even though I met some friends through classes I felt like I had to work to find lab partners and people to be in study groups with. But once I started to push my boundaries and talk to people before and after classes and exchange contact information, it was easier to form study groups. I also went to some social activities and joined the Picnic Day Board of Directors. Now I have met other people, some of whom are now as close as my friends in high school were.
